What we do

Track and Trace
Food Safety is important – 600 million humans are annually falling ill and 420 thousand die from food contamination (WEO); Consumers are more informed, and they demand food traceability; Supply chain optimization and reduction of food waste, as 1/3 of all food produced is lost (WEO); Sustainability goals are open to consumers. By the usage of blockchain technology – aggregation, integration and analysis of data, we create the digital farm connected to society.
Risk-free farm
On the other side, our solution can also be used by banks who will offer loans to farms, insurance companies which will evaluate the farmers’ risk profile and government organisation which need information support. We provide solutions for both software (AI based data analysis) and hardware (precise irrigation, fertilizing tools and introduce robot in sizable farming). In a connected world, the farmer can create a better market position/maximize the sales value by improving efficiency and show its performance on sustainability and food safety criteria.
